## About the Book

In "Fears Harvest," research scientist Audra Vass uncovers a horrifying secret within her high-security workplace: the engineering of terrifying creatures. As an unimaginable evil is unleashed, Audra finds herself trapped in the lab, compelled to confront the grotesque monsters and expose the truth behind their creation.

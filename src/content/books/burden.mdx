## About the Book

"Burden" is a chilling thriller that delves into the darkest corners of the human psyche. Detective Marcus King, newly transferred to a high-crime district, is paired with the enigmatic Owen Barnes. Together, they investigate a series of gruesome murders where victims are arranged in macabre displays, each accompanied by a cryptic message. As they delve deeper into the case, they uncover a sinister pattern that challenges their understanding of justice and morality.

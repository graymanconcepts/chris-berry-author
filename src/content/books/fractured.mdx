## About the Book

In "Fractured," Cassandra seeks an escape from overwhelming mood and anxiety disorders by embracing the revolutionary NeuroSynth technology. This cutting-edge implant promises to elevate her mind to unprecedented heights, offering what is advertised as 'The Perfect Mind.' However, instead of granting her the peace she craves, the NeuroSynth unleashes a series of unforeseen consequences that challenge her perception of reality and identity.

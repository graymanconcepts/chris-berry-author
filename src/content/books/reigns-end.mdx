## About the Book

"Reigns End" is the heart-stopping sequel to "Deniable Actions." The members of Ghost Force grapple with the aftermath of a violent encounter, facing post-traumatic challenges while contending with a rogue agency intent on their downfall. This rollercoaster tale of emotions and action will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

## About the Book

"The Killing Mistake" is a gripping murder mystery that will have you on the edge of your seat. Two detectives are tasked with solving the crimes when two young women are found dead while hitchhiking. As they delve deeper into the case, they discover a web of love, betrayal, and deception that threatens to consume them.
